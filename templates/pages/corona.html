We cordially apologize for the cancellation of Trips due to COVID-19 Pandemic. Himalaya Treks Nepal (HTN) prioritizes full safety, health and well being of the team members and customers. Considering government rules and the fear of rising COVID-19 cases, the CEO and the board of the company have concluded that HTN will only be accessible online. Himalaya Treks Nepal would also like to confirm that bookings for future travel plans are now open. If you are looking or planning for the adventures in the coming days, we would love to hear it from you through email exchanges, Skype, or from our website. Exclusive offers have been made for the customers who were forcibly affected by COVID-19 and who could not complete their trips. Offers:

  ● Everest Base Camp Trek
  ● Annapurna Base CampTrek
  ● Annapurna Circuit Trek
  ● Upper Mustang Trek
  ● Mardi Himal Trek.

Fighting with this pandemic has really been tough these days since it looks like the pandemic is winning this war. However, scientists have proved that effective uses of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) such as masks and gloves, using sanitizers, and maintaining a safe social distance among ourselves can highly reduce the growth of the cases. Therefore, this is a request for the readers to follow these instructions to keep yourself and the surrounding safe.
